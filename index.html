    Hi there! I am now a second-year Master student at the Department of Computational Biology, School of Life Science,Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Huijuan Feng. I received my B.S. degree from East China University of Science and Technology in 2021, where I worked with Prof. Yun Tang and Guixia Liu on the Network Based Pharmacology and AI Based Drug Design.
